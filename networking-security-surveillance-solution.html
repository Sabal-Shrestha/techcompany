Services

Networking and security surveillance solution.

Welcome to our Networking and Security Surveillance Solution service page!

At Etonel, we understand that security is a top priority for any business, no matter the size or industry. That's why we offer a comprehensive networking and security surveillance solution to ensure that your business is protected from all potential threats.

Our networking and security surveillance solution includes the following services:

Network Security Assessment

Our experts will perform a thorough analysis of your current network security infrastructure to identify any vulnerabilities that may exist. We'll provide you with a comprehensive report outlining any security risks and recommended solutions to mitigate these risks.

Firewall Configuration

Our team will configure a customized firewall solution that meets your specific business needs. This will help protect your network from potential security threats such as malware, viruses, and hacking attempts.

VPN Setup

We'll set up a virtual private network (VPN) to enable secure remote access to your business network. This will allow you and your employees to work from anywhere while maintaining the security and integrity of your network.

Surveillance System Installation

We'll install state-of-the-art security cameras and other surveillance equipment to ensure that your business is monitored 24/7. Our team will also configure and maintain the system to ensure optimal performance.

Access Control Systems

We offer a variety of access control systems to restrict entry to specific areas of your business. Our solutions include key card access, biometric scanners, and more.

Security Consulting

Our experts will provide ongoing consulting and support to help you maintain a secure network environment. We'll keep you up-to-date with the latest security threats and recommend any necessary updates or upgrades to your security infrastructure.
